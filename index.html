My work experiences

In the past four years of my study, I have had four different work experiences. Because I am a person who does not want to limit myself to one field, these experiences also prove my continuous attempts in various positions. It is also because of this that I have learned more valuable wealth in these short-term work experiences that I could not feel in the course.

May 2023 – Aug. 2023

Data Analyst Intern

As a data analysis intern last summer at Smartdublin, I successfully developed a preliminary data analysis tool through python, aiding the company in managing and examining stored public data for future use. During this period, I have learned more ways to use Python to handle data models, as well as methods to detect data issues and fill in missing data.

January. 2024 – April. 2024

Undergraduate Researcher

As an undergraduate researcher in Michigan State University, I have actively joined a condensed matter laboratory. Under the guidance of a professor specializing in ultrafast field, I have investigated the atomic structures of different materials and utilized my technical skills to assist the professor with remote intelligent scanning of sample surfaces.

January 2023 - May 2023

Office Assistant

As an office assistant at Michigan State University, my main tasks are to assist faculty and staff in managing and classifying financial expense reports and tax reports for various majors in the Department of Physics, preparing weekly seminars, and preparing appropriate beverages and refreshments in the lounge area. In addition, I also need to organize and print promotional materials for the Department of Physics when necessary, and regularly print midterm or final exams for different physics courses for different professors.

May 2022 - June 2022

Participant

In addition to my main job, I am also keen on off-campus research activities. In 2022, in order to study the severity of water shortage in Las Vegas, my professor and other students who participated in the project went to various attractions in Las Vegas to interview local people and tourists to explore whether people are really aware of the problem of water shortage. I was responsible for editing and shooting the interview video, and this experience also taught me how to edit videos.
